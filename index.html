About Gaza Students Beyond Borders

Gaza Students Beyond Borders was founded in 2020 with the mission of supporting students from Gaza in accessing quality education and professional opportunities around the world. We believe that education is a fundamental right and a powerful tool for positive change.

  • Scholarship and university application assistance
  • Visa and travel support
  • Mentorship programs with professionals worldwide

To date, we have supported over 200 students in their educational journeys across 15 different countries. Our network includes volunteers, professionals, and partner organizations dedicated to creating opportunities for Gaza's youth.
